The topic of my group's statistic research project was what clothing item would sell the most on the market and which brand it should sell to in order to gain the most profit.

A bit of a background as to how it started, we had a completely different topic beforehand which was about the favorite movie genres of highschool students, but wew had a bit of trouble gaining enough data for this so we had decided to find a new data set on a site called kaggle.

The data includes 2 quantitative and 2 qualitative variables which are price & rating, and type of clothing & brand respectively. These variables are what we had deemed most useful for the purpose of our research.
